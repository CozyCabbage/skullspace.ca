Summer of Skullspace Donation Campaign

Summer time is often quiet at Skullspace. Summer 2017, we're going to spice it up with a calendar of events to encourage your donations:

  • Gathering, show and tell every Tuesday at 6pm, with occasional meetings.
  • Monday June 19, 7pm -- Openstreetmap night
  • Wednesday June 28, 7pm-8:30pm -- Resisting the Surveillance State: Three Procurement Choices for Decision Makers. See recording
  • Tuesday July 4, 6pm -- GPG/PGP key exchange / signing.
    Get your GPG/PGP key signed by other Skullspace members. Find out how many degrees of separation you are from other tech celebrities with GPG/PGP keys.
    Come early (5:00-5:45pm) and bring a computer for one on one guidance for creating your keys.
  • Friday July 7, 7:30pm -- Movie Night: How virtual Is Your Reality. 7:30pm eXistenZ, 9:30pm The Lawnmower Man
  • Thursday July 13, 6pm-11pm -- Epic Unix Epoch Event
    The unix epoch rollover to 1500000000 is happening on July 13 at 9:40:00 PM CDT - come have a fun "time"!
  • Sunday July 16, 2pm -- Introduction to QubesOS -- short presentation, install workshop and live media (CDs, DVDs, install to your flash drives) available by donation.
  • Monday July 31, 7pm -- iPXE (netboot) fun with Skullspace desks
  • Tuesday August 8, 6pm -- Skullspace bitcoin night -- end of campaign.
    Come find out how much the campaign raised and see the matching confirmed. Buy and sell bitcoin for cash with other members in an open market setting. Open auction floor for items for bitcoin. Discussion on the state of bitcoin and related tech. Showing of relevant documentary or conference talks, selection TBA.

All events are at Skullspace, 2-374 Donald St in Winnipeg and open to the public. Entering the building may require buzzing the "sksp" doorbell and waiting for a member to come open the door.

Want to put on your own event this summer? Contact Mark Jenkins mark@parit.ca

Donate and Get Matched!

Like Skullspace and our 2017 summer calendar? Support our summer 2017 donation campaign. All donations to Skullspace between June 17, 2017 and August 8, 2017 will be matched $1 for $1 by a combination of:

  • Our bitcoin endowment fund -- $325
  • An additional donor pledging $500 worth of bitcoin
This means if we raise $825 there will be an additional $825 matched and the campaign will raise $1650.
